Tofu Scramble

A delicious recipe for you.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

servings

2 servings

instructions

Tips

You can add your favorite vegetables like onions, bell peppers and mushrooms to the scramble at your liking. Be sure to sautee them first. You can add cream cheese or almond milk to make the scramble extra fluffy!

step 1

Press the block of tofu between paper towels to squeeze out water.

step 2

Place 2 quart pot over low to medium heat. Add a teaspoon of your preferred cooking oil to the pan. (We like coconut or olive oil)

step 3

Crumble tofu into the pan. Mix in all of the seasonings.

step 4

Reduce temperature and cook for 5-8 minutes, stirring occassionally. (it's very easy to overcook)

step 5

Remove from heat and serve. I like to have mine with lighly buttered toast.

ingredients

8oz Extra Firm Tofu

1/4 Tspn Turmeric Powder

1/4 Tspn Himalayan Black Salt

1/8 Tspn Onion and Garlic Powder

1 Tbspn Nutritional Yeast (for cheesiness)

Dashes of black pepper and oregano

tools

Paper Towels

2qt Pot

Measuring spoons
